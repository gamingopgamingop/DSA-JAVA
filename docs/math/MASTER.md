# 📐 Mathematics Knowledge Module

The foundational language for all technology.

## 📌 Core Taxonomy

### 1. Discrete Mathematics
- **Logic**: Propositional and First-Order Logic.
- **Set Theory**: Basics of collections and relations.
- **Graph Theory**: Trees, paths, cycles, network flow.
- **Combinatorics**: Counting, permutations, combinations.

### 2. Calculus & Analysis
- **Differential**: Derivatives, Gradients (foundational for AI).
- **Integral**: Integration, areas under curves.
- **Optimization**: Gradient Descent, Lagrange Multipliers.

### 3. Linear Algebra
- **Vectors & Matrices**: Transformations, eigenvalues, eigenvectors.
- **Decompositions**: SVD, PCA (dimensionality reduction).

### 4. Probability & Statistics
- **Distributions**: Normal, Bernoulli, Poisson.
- **Inference**: Bayesian vs. Frequentist, Hypothesis testing.

---

## 🔗 Interconnections
- **Math ↔ AI**: Gradient descent in ML is a **Calculus** optimization problem.
- **Math ↔ CS**: Cryptography relies heavily on **Number Theory** (Prime numbers, modular arithmetic).
- **Math ↔ DSA**: Algorithm analysis uses **Combinatorics** and **Recurrence Relations**.

---

## 🚀 Learning Path
1. **Algebra & Geometry** (The tools).
2. **Calculus & Linear Algebra** (The engines of AI).
3. **Probability & Discrete Math** (The language of CS).
