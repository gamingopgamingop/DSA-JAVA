# 📂 Global Codebase & Implementation Resources

This document maps the primary repositories and platforms for accessing implementations of the 200,000+ algorithms and 2.5M+ AI models cataloged in our system.

---

## 🛠️ Multi-Language Algorithm Repositories

### 1. [Rosetta Code](https://rosettacode.org/)
- **Scope**: 1,000+ programming tasks implemented in hundreds of languages.
- **Sorting Focus**: Documents **47 distinct sorting algorithm** implementations.
- **Utility**: Excellent for comparing how the same algorithm is expressed in different paradigms (Imperative vs. Functional).

### 2. [The Algorithms](https://the-algorithms.com/)
- **Scope**: The world's largest open-source algorithm library.
- **Language Hubs**:
  - [Java](https://github.com/TheAlgorithms/Java)
  - [Python](https://github.com/TheAlgorithms/Python)
  - [C++](https://github.com/TheAlgorithms/C-Plus-Plus)
- **Coverage**: Comprehensive coverage of DSA, Machine Learning, and Mathematics.

---

## 🤖 AI & Machine Learning Hubs

### 1. [Hugging Face](https://huggingface.co/)
- **Models**: **2.5 Million+ public AI models** (as of 2025).
- **Datasets**: 150,000+ datasets for training and evaluation.
- **Libraries**: Transformers, Diffusers, Accelerate.

### 2. [Papers with Code](https://paperswithcode.com/)
- **Scope**: Links research papers directly to their GitHub implementations.
- **Statistics**: Over 100,000+ papers with codebases.
- **Benchmarks**: Tracks SOTA (State-of-the-Art) performance across thousands of tasks.

---

## 🔢 Mathematical & Scientific Computing

### 1. [NIST Digital Library of Mathematical Functions (DLMF)](https://dlmf.nist.gov/)
- **Scope**: Definitive resource for mathematical formulas, identities, and proofs.

### 2. [Netlib](https://www.netlib.org/)
- **Scope**: Collection of mathematical software, papers, and databases (LAPACK, BLAS).
